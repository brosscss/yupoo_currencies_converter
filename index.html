Privacy Policy for Yupoo Currencies Converter

1. Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy for Yupoo currencies converter. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy will explain how we collect, use, and protect the data associated with our extension.

2. Data We Collect

The only data we collect from you is your currency preference. This is essential for the primary function of our extension, which is to convert prices from yuan to your chosen currency.

3. How We Use the Data

We use your currency preference to fetch real-time conversion rates from the "exchangerate" API. This ensures that the prices displayed on Yupoo are converted to your preferred currency.

4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not share or transfer your data with any third parties. The currency preference you provide remains within the extension and is only used for the purpose of currency conversion.

5. Data Storage

Your currency preference is stored locally within the extension's settings. It is not stored on any external servers or databases.

6. Security

We have implemented measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at cssbross@gmail.com.
